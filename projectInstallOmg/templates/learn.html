How to read the table on CSUEB website

  • Ignore the column names and row names
  • Try to find the days your class meets in the table keeping in mind they are organized by the start time of classes
  • Once you find your class, then look at the column name you found it in, that's the date of your final, and the row is the time of your final
Or use this table
  • Find the days your class meets in the columns
  • Fine the time that your class starts in the rows
  • Like the highlighted example, you can find the time and date of your exam
    • Eg: A class meets on Tuesdays and Thursdays at 8:30 am
